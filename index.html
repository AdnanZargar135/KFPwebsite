About Kashmir Fruit Preservers

Excellence in Fruit Preservation

Located in the picturesque region of Lassipora, Pulwama, Kashmir Fruit Preservers stands as a beacon of excellence in the apple preservation industry. Our state-of-the-art Controlled Atmosphere (CA) storage facility is designed to maintain the freshness, quality, and nutritional value of Kashmir's world-renowned apples.

With multiple CA chambers equipped with cutting-edge technology, we ensure optimal storage conditions specifically tailored for different apple varieties. Our world-class grading lines guarantee that only the highest quality Kashmir apples reach our customers, maintaining the premium reputation of our region's finest produce.

Kashmir apples are our specialty - from the crisp Red Delicious to the sweet Golden Delicious varieties. Our cooperative and dedicated staff work tirelessly to preserve these premium apples, ensuring customer satisfaction at every step of the process.
